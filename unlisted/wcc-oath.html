I, [Name], solemnly affirm that I, in the name of the Courtroom, will keep it secure;

that I will not disclose anything linked to it to any non-Courtroom members;

that I accept the welcoming people of the courtroom as they have accepted me;

that I will contribute for the good of discourse, ideas, speech, and humor;

that even after this Courtroom is long gone or that I have departed from it I will not compromise it;

that I will act decently*;

that I am someone who the Courtroom made a good decision in collectively trusting;

that tolerance is a virtue I possess**;

that I acknowledge that I am essential to its integrity;

and lastly that in my eyes and in those of everyone else, it does not exist.

*Definitions may vary, we're very chill though.
**Just have a minimum of tolerance.

Please note: the oath can only be administered after a vote, if more than half the votes are in favor of adding the person, then it can be administered by anyone, so long as there's a consensus on whoever does it THE OATH CAN THEREFORE NOT BE ADMINISTERED WITHOUT A POSITIVE VOTE.
