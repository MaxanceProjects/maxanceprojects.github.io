Current Update: 4.0 - Saturday October 14th 2023. For more information, consult the change log below:

You may see that the website may offer translations. This is currently not the case right now but it's a work in progress. When on a page, the translation that you're on and the ones that are available will be more visible than the others (e.g white and have a hover effect unlike the gray ones). Website History

Coding began as early as September 2020, however I spent very little time on it, beginning the wiki and press sections (still a work in progress). Fast.io was considered in December where developpement gained some speed. On August 12th 2021 the website has been launched in its first version, hosting an index, the about page, an exploring portal with four sections, and a works portal which currently doesn't host anything. However, at the current moment, some unfinished components can be hidden in the website, those will be available soon.
