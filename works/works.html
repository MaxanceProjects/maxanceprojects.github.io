Maxance's Laws

Originally known as "Maxance's Laws Of Motion" (a parody of Newton's laws, but also Kepler's, and other scientists). these are supposed to be as objective as possible, however most are considered unusual and/or useless. Some are take their roots in math, others are worded, others are an uncanny amalgamation of both. There are also additional points like mathematical projects and analysis, explanations for the process, and more.
